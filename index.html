Who We Are

TECA (B) Sdn Bhd is a registered Public Works Department Class IV specialist contractor providing services in the field of geotechnical, geological, geophysical, environmental and ground engineering.


Incorporated in 1988 in Brunei Darussalam, our company operates a laboratory equipped to carry out a wide range of soil, concrete/material, coarse aggregates, bricks, water and cement tests. Our laboratory is one of the few laboratories accredited to ISO/IEC 17025 by the Ministry of Development in Brunei Darussalam.


Our highly experienced personnel employ cutting-edge equipment to carry out field and laboratory tests for civil engineering works across both public and private sectors in Brunei Darussalam, including the construction and oil and gas industries.
